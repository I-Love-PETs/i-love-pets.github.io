# Industry Pain Points

## The Itch

Teams want PETs that survive procurement, security review, latency budgets, and messy data.

## Why It Matters

A PET that works only in a paper or demo will not change privacy outcomes in production.

## Current State

Real deployments struggle with integration, monitoring, explainability, cost, and unclear ownership of privacy claims.

## What Is Unsolved

Better implementation playbooks, deployment benchmarks, failure-mode catalogs, and operator-friendly controls.

## Possible Directions

Document reference architectures, measure developer effort, publish runbooks, and collect deployment postmortems.

## Difficulty

Intermediate.

## Good For

Privacy engineers, architects, maintainers, and product teams.

## Related Patterns

All patterns, benchmarks, deployments.
