# PET Composition

## The Itch

Real systems combine PETs, but end-to-end guarantees are often unclear.

## Why It Matters

FL plus DP plus MPC, FL plus TEE, or HE plus DP can be stronger than any one PET, but only if assumptions compose.

## Current State

Most documentation explains PETs one at a time. Builders need composition guidance.

## What Is Unsolved

End-to-end guarantees, failure-mode interactions, privacy accounting across components, and usable architecture reviews.

## Possible Directions

Create composition checklists, model assumption dependencies, and analyze common stacks such as FL + DP + secure aggregation.

## Difficulty

Advanced.

## Good For

Architects, researchers, auditors, and platform teams.

## Related Patterns

Cross-silo FL, private inference, confidential inference, DP synthetic data.
