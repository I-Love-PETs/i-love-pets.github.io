# Good First Research Problems

## The Itch

New contributors need tractable PET problems that are useful but not thesis-sized.

## Why It Matters

PETs need better entry ramps. Good first problems build the community and produce reusable evaluation artifacts.

## Current State

Many lists point to papers. Fewer explain what experiment, benchmark, or implementation would be useful next.

## What Is Unsolved

Reliable small benchmarks, threat-model examples, reproducible attack demos, and usability studies are underdeveloped.

## Possible Directions

Build a gradient-leakage demo suite, compare DP accounting explanations, write PSI output-leakage examples, or create a TEE attestation UX study.

## Difficulty

Beginner to intermediate.

## Good For

Students, open-source contributors, reading groups, and maintainers.

## Related Patterns

Federated analytics, cross-silo FL, confidential inference.
