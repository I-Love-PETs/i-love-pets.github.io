# Differential Privacy

## The Itch

DP has strong theory but remains hard to use, explain, audit, and tune for modern AI systems.

## Why It Matters

Teams need guarantees that are both mathematically meaningful and operationally understandable.

## Current State

Tooling exists, but privacy accounting, parameter selection, and utility evaluation remain specialist-heavy.

## What Is Unsolved

DP for LLMs, accounting usability, DP auditing, and utility/privacy optimization.

## Possible Directions

Build accounting explainers, compare DP-SGD auditing methods, study privacy budgets for fine-tuning, and develop task-specific utility reports.

## Difficulty

Intermediate to advanced.

## Good For

Privacy researchers, ML engineers, and auditors.

## Related Patterns

DP synthetic data release, private LLM fine-tuning, benchmarks.
