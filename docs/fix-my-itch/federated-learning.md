# Federated Learning

## The Itch

Federated learning promises collaboration without centralizing data, but production systems still struggle with non-IID data, poisoning, personalization, communication cost, and leakage from updates.

## Why It Matters

These problems decide whether FL is a practical privacy architecture or just a data-movement story.

## Current State

Secure aggregation and DP help, but they complicate debugging, robustness, and utility.

## What Is Unsolved

Non-IID learning, privacy-preserving personalization, poisoning resistance under secure aggregation, efficient communication, and gradient leakage diagnostics.

## Possible Directions

Create benchmark suites with realistic silo imbalance, robust aggregation compatible with secure aggregation, and update-leakage test harnesses.

## Difficulty

Intermediate to advanced.

## Good For

ML researchers, privacy engineers, and distributed systems builders.

## Related Patterns

Cross-silo federated learning, FL secure aggregation, FL differential privacy.
