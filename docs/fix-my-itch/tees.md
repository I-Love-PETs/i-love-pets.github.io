# TEEs

## The Itch

TEEs offer practical confidential computing, but trust, side channels, and attestation usability remain difficult.

## Why It Matters

Many teams can deploy TEEs sooner than heavier cryptography, but only if users can understand and rely on the guarantees.

## Current State

Cloud offerings are maturing, yet multi-cloud portability and operational assurance are uneven.

## What Is Unsolved

Side-channel resilience, attestation usability, and portable confidential-computing patterns.

## Possible Directions

Create attestation UX patterns, compare cloud enclave workflows, document side-channel mitigations, and test composability with DP.

## Difficulty

Intermediate to advanced.

## Good For

Security engineers, cloud architects, and privacy platform teams.

## Related Patterns

Confidential inference, confidential RAG, side channels.
