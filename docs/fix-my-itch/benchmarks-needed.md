# Benchmarks Needed

## The Itch

PET decisions lack shared benchmarks for privacy, utility, cost, latency, robustness, scalability, and developer effort.

## Why It Matters

Without benchmarks, teams compare claims rather than evidence.

## Current State

Benchmarks exist in fragments, often tied to one paper, one library, or one narrow workload.

## What Is Unsolved

Standardized workloads, realistic threat models, cost reporting, and reproducible deployment conditions.

## Possible Directions

Build benchmark cards, publish reference workloads, include developer-effort measures, and track privacy attack performance.

## Difficulty

Beginner to advanced.

## Good For

Maintainers, researchers, funders, and practitioners.

## Related Patterns

Benchmarks, deployments, all pattern pages.
