# Healthcare Deployments

## Problem

Multi-site research and model development need broader data than one institution can provide.

## Architecture

Cross-silo federated learning or federated analytics with institutional participants and a coordinating body.

## PETs Used

Federated learning, secure aggregation, DP, PSI, synthetic data, and clean-room governance.

## What Worked

Local data control aligns with institutional governance and can lower the barrier to collaboration.

## Challenges

Non-IID data, clinical validation, small cohorts, consent boundaries, and operational burden.

## Lessons Learned

Define the privacy unit, validation plan, and output policy before training starts.
