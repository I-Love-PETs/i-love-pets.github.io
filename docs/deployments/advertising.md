# Advertising Deployments

## Problem

Measurement and attribution require collaboration across platforms, advertisers, and publishers.

## Architecture

Clean rooms, PSI joins, federated measurement, and DP thresholds for aggregate outputs.

## PETs Used

Clean rooms, PSI, DP, MPC, and federated analytics.

## What Worked

Governed workflows and aggregate reporting fit many business needs.

## Challenges

Identity normalization, small segments, repeated-query leakage, and platform trust.

## Lessons Learned

Clean-room governance should be paired with technical output controls and repeated-query analysis.
