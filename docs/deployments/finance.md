# Finance Deployments

## Problem

Institutions need shared signals for fraud, compliance, and risk while protecting customer data.

## Architecture

PSI or MPC-backed collaboration with audit controls and strict output policy.

## PETs Used

PSI, MPC, TEEs, clean rooms, and DP for aggregate reporting.

## What Worked

Narrow, high-value computations are easier to operationalize than broad data-sharing programs.

## Challenges

Malicious incentives, auditability, regulatory review, latency, and schema alignment.

## Lessons Learned

Treat output leakage and participant incentives as first-class architecture concerns.
