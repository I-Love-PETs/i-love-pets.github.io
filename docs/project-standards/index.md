# Project Standards

I ❤️ PETs is a curated field guide. Its value comes from judgment that readers can inspect, not from collecting every link.

## v0.2 Focus

The v0.2 goal is to make the guide trustworthy enough for real decision support.

That means:

- Fewer unsupported tradeoff claims
- More dated and sourced judgment
- Clearer primary reader
- Internal decision paths that connect pages
- A visible maintenance model for fast-moving claims

## Editorial Rule

Every decision-support page should help a reader answer:

> What should I believe, why should I believe it, what might have changed, and what should I verify before building?

## What Counts As Progress

Adding another page is less valuable than improving a page's trustworthiness. Prefer one well-evidenced tradeoff table over five generic summaries.
