# AI

## Decision Context

AI systems introduce privacy risk through training data, prompts, embeddings, retrieved context, evaluations, and generated outputs.

## Useful PETs

FL and DP can support training. TEEs and HE can support inference. Confidential RAG can reduce exposure of retrieval context.

## Watch Outs

Model behavior is hard to bound. Privacy review should include memorization, extraction, membership inference, logging, and downstream sharing.
